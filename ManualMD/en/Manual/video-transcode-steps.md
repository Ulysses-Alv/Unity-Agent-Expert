# Transcode your video files

To enable transcoding for your video files, you need to change the settings in the Video Clip Importer settings. Follow these steps:

## Access and configure the transcoding settings

To transcode your video files:

1. Select your [video clip asset](video-clips-use.md). The **Inspector** window shows the **Video Clip Import Settings**.
2. Enable **Transcode**.
3. Configure the transcoding settings in the [Video Clip Import Settings](class-VideoClip.md#transcode-options).
4. Once you’re satisfied with your settings, select **Apply**.

Unity transcodes your video file with your new settings.
