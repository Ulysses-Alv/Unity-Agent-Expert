# Advanced sculpting tools

**Note:** These tools are only available in the [Terrain Tools package](https://docs.unity3d.com/Packages/com.unity.terrain-tools@latest/).

The advanced sculpting tools automate some complex sculpting tasks. The tools are:

| **Tool** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| [**Add noise to your terrain**](terrain-Sculpt-Noise-Landing.md) | Adds noise or randomness to the **terrain**. |
| [**Build a terrace**](terrain-Sculpt-Terrace.md) | Creates a stepped effect on the terrain. |
| [**Clone a part of the terrain**](terrain-Sculpt-Clone.md) | Copies a section of terrain and pastes it elsewhere. |
| [**Build a bridge between two points**](terrain-Sculpt-Bridge.md) | Creates a bridge between two areas of terrain. |
