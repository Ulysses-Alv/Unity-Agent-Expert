# Playing video in Movie Textures

Resources for importing a video file into Unity as an animated texture, and playing it back.

**Note:** MovieTexture is due to be deprecated in a future version of Unity. You should use [VideoPlayer](../ScriptReference/Video.VideoPlayer.md) for video download and movie playback.

| **Page** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| [Create a movie texture](MovieTexture-create.md) | Convert a video file to a Movie Texture asset, and play the video via a script. |
| [Play video on mobile platforms](MovieTexture-mobile.md) | Play video on platforms that don’t support Movie Textures. |
