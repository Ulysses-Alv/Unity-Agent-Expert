# Post Processing

[com.unity.postprocessing](https://docs.unity3d.com/Packages/com.unity.postprocessing@3.5/manual/index.html) ![](../uploads/Main/iconRel.png)

## Description

The post-processing stack (v2) comes with a collection of effects and image filters you can apply to your cameras to improve the visuals of your games.
