# Compound colliders

Compound colliders are collections of multiple colliders on a single **Rigidbody** **GameObject**. Each **collider** is attached to a child GameObject, with the Rigidbody GameObject as its parent. This allows you to group and arrange colliders into complex shapes, and have the physics system treat them as a single [rigid body](RigidbodiesOverview.md).
