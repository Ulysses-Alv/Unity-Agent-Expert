# Focus searches with search providers

Search providers divide the search into categories like project assets, settings, and packages. They focus your search by excluding results that aren’t relevant to your current task. This section describes how to use search providers to filter your search results. For information on individual search providers, refer to [Search provider references](search-by-provider.md) section.
